Positions: ⬇️

Goalkeeper

  • Plays in goal
  • Is allowed to touch the ball with his hands
  • 1 per team
  • Requires fast reflexes, reaction time and defensive skills

Defender

  • Plays in the back
  • 3-4 per team
  • Requires defensive skills, strong physis and great passing

Midfielder

  • Plays in the center
  • 3-6 per team
  • Requires speed, great passing and decently good playmaking skills

Attacker

  • Plays in the front
  • 1-4 per team
  • Requires speed, amazing dribbeling skills and a great shot
